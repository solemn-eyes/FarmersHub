About the Farm

GreenYard Poultry has been a family-run farm focused on sustainable, humane poultry farming. We started with a small flock and grew by prioritizing ethical practices and high-quality feed. Our mission is to provide healthy poultry and farm products to our neighbours while supporting local agriculture.
